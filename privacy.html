Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 03/11/26 · Effective Date: 03/11/26

1. Introduction

Welcome to Study Room ("we," "us," "our," or the "Company"). Study Room is a mobile application that allows users to timelapse themselves studying and share sessions with small accountability groups called "Rooms." This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By accessing or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not access or use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and profile avatar/photo.
  • Authentication Data: If you sign in via Google or Apple Sign-In, we receive your name, email, and profile photo URL. We do not receive or store your third-party passwords.
  • Profile Information: Display name, profile picture, and any other information you add.
  • Study Session Data: Subject labels, session duration, start and end times.
  • Room Information: Room names, descriptions, and membership details.
  • Reactions and Interactions: Emoji reactions and session view records.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Camera Data: During an active session, the front-facing camera captures periodic image frames to create a timelapse. Camera access is only active during sessions you explicitly start.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and unique identifiers for push notifications.
  • Push Notification Tokens: If you grant permission, your device's push token is collected.
  • Usage Data: Session timestamps, streak info, total study minutes, and interaction patterns.

2.3 Information NOT Collected

We do not collect geolocation data, audio, microphone access, contacts, calendar, financial data, or use tracking technologies for advertising.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App and its features
  • To enable room-based social accountability features
  • To track and display your study progress and streaks
  • To send push notifications (with your permission)
  • To improve performance and user experience
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. How Your Timelapse Data Works

  • Frame Capture: Frames are captured from your front camera at regular intervals (~every 10 seconds).
  • On-Device Processing: Frames are stored temporarily on your device and stitched into a timelapse entirely on-device.
  • Upload: Once you review and approve, the final video is uploaded to our secure servers.
  • Access Control: Videos are only viewable by members of Rooms you choose to share with, via time-limited signed URLs.
  • Deletion: Individual frames are deleted after stitching. You can delete sessions and timelapses at any time.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share only in these limited circumstances:

5.1 With Other Room Members

Your display name, avatar, session data, timelapse, and streak data are visible to members of Rooms you belong to — not the general public.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Encryption in Transit: All data is encrypted using TLS/SSL.
  • Secure Authentication: Tokens are stored via platform-native secure storage (Keychain / encrypted Android storage).
  • Row-Level Security: Database enforces RLS policies — users only access authorized data.
  • Signed URLs: Timelapse videos require time-limited signed URLs for access.

No method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account.
  • Sessions and Timelapses: Retained until you delete them or your account.
  • Reactions and Views: Retained as long as the associated session exists.

Upon account deletion, your personal data is deleted or anonymized within 30 days.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: View your data through your profile and session history.
  • Correction: Update your display name and avatar in profile settings.
  • Deletion: Delete individual sessions or request full account deletion.
  • Camera Permissions: Grant or revoke at any time via device settings.
  • Push Notifications: Enable or disable via device or App settings.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data by contacting us.

9. Rights for Users in Specific Jurisdictions

EEA / United Kingdom — GDPR

If you are in the EEA or UK, you have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and data portability. Contact us to exercise any of these rights.

California — CCPA/CPRA

California residents have rights including the right to know, delete, opt-out of sale (we do not sell data), and non-discrimination. Contact us to exercise these rights.

10. Children's Privacy

Study Room is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using the App, you consent to such transfers. We take appropriate safeguards to protect your data.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy
